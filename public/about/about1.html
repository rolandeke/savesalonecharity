ABOUT US

Save Sierra Leone Charity Foundation

Save Sierra Leone Charity Foundation is an independent organization for the poor and needy. We aim at bringing a real and lasting change to families living in poverty. In partnership with contributors and sponsors , we reduce their daily struggles, invest in their potential, and provide them with the opportunity to grow up healthy, educated and prepared to succeed and contribute to society
